Rules for Swash

View rules on your phone:

Setup

  • All players receive: 3 Lumber, 4 Leather, 2 Iron, 2 Coffee, 1 Rum
  • Choose a player to give the 1st Player Token
  • Separate Small, Medium, and Large ships. Make an active draw deck with the Small ships and set the other 2 decks aside.
  • Give each player their starting ship & cards for their faction, 5 armor/weapon tokens, along with the 5 crew and damage cubes of their color.

Game Phase

The game consists of a series of rounds. In each round:

  • Discard & Draw Ships up to the active player count - 1 (max 4)
  • All players reshuffle discarded cards to form new draw pile
  • Turn Phases
  • Upkeep Phase
  • Combat Phase
  • Buy Phase
  • Pass 1st Player Token one place clockwise

Game End & Scoring

The game ends when a player has all 3 Captain abilities unlocked and conquers 1 additional ship

Scoring:

  • Treasures: Add up treasure values
  • Resources: Flip over all resources and add up the values underneath
  • Ship: Add the following victory points based on your current ship:
    • Sloop: 1
    • Clipper: 2
    • Schooner: 3
    • Brig: 5
    • Frigate: 6
    • Galleon: 8
    • Man-of-War: 10

Additional Phases

Turn Phase:

  • Redraw up to 3 cards total
  • Perform one of the following actions with 1-3 cards (or pass):
    • Play (top action) - If card says attack, see: Attack Phase
    • Upgrade Captain or Ship (bottom action)
      • The player may use this to replace a Captain Upgrade of the same type, or any existing Ship Upgrade.
      • Note for Boarding Hook: When placing free crewmember, place one of your damage cubes as a marker to remember not to pay upkeep this round.
    • Discard for resource identified in top-left corner
      • Note: a player must perform one top/bottom action per turn, or else they pass and may not play the rest of their cards
  • If a player passes, they may not draw any more cards in this round
  • Go to next player
  • Continue turn phases until all players have passed

Upkeep Phase:

  • For any open crew spot on your captain sheet, pay the associated cost identified by the revealed icon.
  • If you do not have that specific resource, you may use one or more resources if they add up to a value greater than the required resource.
  • If you are not able to pay, or choose not to, return one crewmember from any location back to your captain sheet.

Attack Phase:

  • If attacking a Ship, ship attacks back (against origin of attack - Captain or Ship)
  • Roll # of FATE Dice listed and add to CV for damage
  • If greater than Defense, Ship is damaged/Captain is injured
  • Damaged ships may not attack/be upgraded until next round. Place one black damage cube on the ship.
  • Injured captains flip over one ability, if one exists. Place one black damage cube on the captain sheet.
    • If the captain receives 4 injuries, they are dead and the player is removed from the game!
  • If no ability to flip, Captain may not attack/be upgraded until next round

Combat Phase:

For each ship with damage cubes:

  • Roll amount of FATE dice listed and add the roll to the damage (total damage is damage cubes + roll)
    • If total damage >= Sink DMG, the ship is sunk and lost. Anyone with a crewmember and/or damage cube on the ship receives the first resource listed under Ship Rewards.
    • If total damage >= health, the ship is conquered.
  • If conquered, the player with the most crew will take the ship
    • If tied in crew (or zero crew), the player with the most damage cubes take the ship
    • If tied on damage, each player will roll 4 FATE dice (repeat until a player has a higher number)
  • The player who conquered the ship may chose one of 2 options:
    • Use Ship:
      • Discard your current ship
      • Discard all current upgrades and take resources equal to their value (top left corner)
      • Place current ship in play area
    • Scrap Ship:
      • Receive rewards listed in "Scrap Rewards"
      • Receive treasure (draw X, keep 1):
        • Small Ship: X == 1
        • Medium Ship: X == 2
        • Large Ship: X == 3
      • Discard treasures down to cargo limit
      • Discard scrapped ship
  • The player who conquered the ship may choose 1 ability for their Captain
    • They may not take an ability for a body part with an existing ability
    • Place the new ability face-up (uninjured side up)
  • Damage cubes are returned to players and crew are placed back on Captain sheet
  • When a ship is conquered, shuffle in the next largest ship deck, if any are left

Buy Phase:

Starting with 1st player:

  • Barter (one-time action):
    • Allows you to remove an unwanted card from the buy area, and optionally trade for resources
    • Move the card to the discard pile next to the buy deck
    • Optionally, trade resources with a total value up to the value of the resource in the top-left corner of a card in the buy area
  • Buy (repeated action):
    • Pay resources identified in the middle area to take a card and place in your discard
    • Replace the card bought with one from the deck immediately after buying
    • May do this as many times as resources allow
